About SpaceCAT

SpaceCAT is an application which allows users to view NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD)!

Go to the 'Search APOD' link within the 'APOD' dropdown and simply enter a date to find photos.

You can save your favourites photos and add them to albums. You can save as many pictures as you want and add them to as many albums as you would like too. Go have fun exploring space!
